Client Portfolio

This is a graphic artist I am friends with, and used to work for. She wanted a design that showed her skills, as well as a look that showed her love for painting.

I went for canvas tones for the background, and used her favorite color, and black for accents. I then used slide-in animations to emulate brush strokes for areas of the layout, as well as thin brush strokes in place of the standard skill graphs.
