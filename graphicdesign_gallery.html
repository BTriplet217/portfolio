Mom's Transplant

For my mother's lung transplant donations, we decided a good way to earn donations was by selling tshirts. For this, we needed a good logo, and I wanted to show that it is for a lung transplant for someone who is very much loved.

I went for a pair of lungs, with a heart in the middle to show this message. I used purples and pinks as they have been known to symbolize hope, compassion, and love. The message at the bottom was mom's personal add-on, which is something she has to tell people often, who don't understand what she is going through.




Bar & Grill

A bar and grill concept a friend and I thought of one night, based on our last names: Triplet, and Spiller. While we do not condone drinking and driving, the name is meant to elude the actions many people have when drinking.

This, while mostly for fun, is a business we one day may attempt to fulfill. In the meantime, we just have a fun logo, that could be worn on a shirt for a pride parade.

Brawling Okras

A fun parody design based loosely by the Scottsdale Fighting Artichokes. I always loved the idea of Oklahoma having a fun mascot, and decided an Okra named Okie would be perfect! To stay in the realm of competition, I named this fantasy team The Brawling Okras.




Solar System

As a huge lover of space, I wanted to design a solar system design to print on a blanket. This took a bit of time to get sizing better relevant and colors correct, but overall was just a fun project.
