D&D Tracking Website

As someone who can find it difficult sifting through bunches of papers on different characters, monsters, and locations, I've started to design a Dungeons and Dragons website to log and keep all my campaign information. Once I discussed with my Dungeon Master (the game manager), and my team, they gave me insights on new things I could add and keep every portion of our campaign on one website.

Currently, it has a character sheet to track progress and keep up with different elements of the game, as well as a fully functional dice roller. While this is nowhere near done, it will show promising changes and adjustments as the design progresses.
